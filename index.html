Measuring \( \delta \)

geometrical inputs + measured dpc value P:

\[ \delta = \frac{2 \pi p \tan \theta}{D}P \]

\( p \) period of the grating
\( D \) distance \(G_1-G_2\)
\( \theta \) wedge angle
\( P \) measured DPC value
